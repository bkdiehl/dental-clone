MEET DR. DAN UTLEY

Dan Utley, DDS

Dr Dan Utley received his degree in Dentistry from the University of Nebraska in 1986, where he graduated #1 in his class in Clinical Performance. He received awards for his outstanding abilities in Operative Dentistry and Endodontics. He also received the award as the Outstanding Graduate in the General Practice Program, and was selected to be a member of Omicron Kappa Upsilon-an honor given only to the top 5% of graduating dentists.

He is a member of the American Dental Association and the Utah Dental Association. He is also a member and past president of the Southern Utah Dental Society.

Dr Utley was born and raised in Richfield, Utah. He married the former Patricia Valantine and they have eight children. He attended Brigham Young University and served a mission for the L.D.S. church in Portugal.

Dr. Utley enjoys spending time with his family hiking, camping, boating, and riding four-wheelers. His other hobbies include scuba diving, playing racquetball, and racing cars.

Dr. Utley is very personable. He has a high level of integrity and a true passion for dentistry. He particularly has a talent for cosmetic dentistry and complex dental cases. Let him help you improve your dental health and brighten your smile.

Credentials:

  • Graduate University of Nebraska School of Dentistry, DDS
  • Undergraduate BYU-Brigham Young University
  • Lumineers Certified Dentist
  • Certification on 3 types of Dental Lasers
  • DOCS certified dentist-oral conscious sedation
  • Advanced Training in Implant Placement and Bone Grafting Procedures

Memberships:

  • ADA-American Dental Association
  • UDA-Utah Dental Association
